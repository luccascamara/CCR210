Caminho Workspace:
home/robot/workspace

"Ativar as ferramentas ROS" no terminal:
source /opt/ros/humble/setup.bash

Abrir o Gazebo:
ros2 launch turtlebot3_gazebo empty_world.launch.py x_pose:=-8

Abrir um mapa:
bash worlds/room_with_obstacles.sh

Casa do TurtleBot:
ros2 launch turtlebot3_gazebo turtlebot3_house.launch.py

Cartographer:
ros2 launch turtlebot3_cartographer cartographer.launch.py

Teleop:
ros2 run turtlebot3_teleop teleop_keyboard

Salvar o Mapa:
ros2 run nav2_map_server map_saver_cli
